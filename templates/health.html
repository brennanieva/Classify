Health

Sleep
According to the CDC young adults need at least seven hours of sleep per night. Although activities such as socializing and schoolwork may make this difficult, a lack of sleep has many consequences such as lack of productivity, caffeine dependencies and more.

Exercise
Did you know that high GPAs and exercise are linked? Elevated moods and lower stress levels are just the half of the reasons why. It is recommended for university-aged students to exercise at least two hours per week at the bare minimum but the more they do the better.

Drinking Water
Our bodies are almost entirely made up of water. So why not replenish it? Adults should drink at least eight ounces of water every day. Water is much healthier than other beverage alternatives which are often high in sugar and calories.
